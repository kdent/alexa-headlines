Short Skill Description: Listen to the top story headlines from The New York
Times.

Full Skill Description: Headlines provides you with the top stories from the New York Times from a variety of sections. Simply ask for the top stories in the section you're interested in. The data is provided by The New York Times, but The New York Times is not affiliated with this skill. 

Example Phrases:
Alexa, ask headlines for the top stories
Alexa, ask headlines what the top stories in technology are
Alexa, ask headlines for the top stories in business

Category: News

Keywords: NYT, news, headlines

Testing Instructions: none
